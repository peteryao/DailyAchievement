Replacing the impossible with the possible.






Whether you're a jack-of-all-trades or just an average joe trying to learn some skills, Life Quest can help you achieve your own victory.







An achievement a day, exceed your own expectations~




There’s a dreamer inside all of us. Whether or not you’re dreaming of learning a new language, losing ten pounds, or just dreaming of a new, creative way to compete with friends, Life Quest can help you grasp your dreams.

Competition

Compete with others as you collectively attempt to make our leaderboards. The top 20 questers will be publicly showcased on the leaderboard. Show everyone who’s boss. (Hint: It’s you.)

Knowledge

Incentive to finally finish your damn essay. Or learn the language you’ve always been meaning to. Or learn how to flambe to impress that date that you have next week, custom quests will give you that extra push that you need to finally start checking off that mental to-do list.

Fun

Invite your friends and compete with them head-to-head. Set quests for you and your friends to complete together. See who can be the first to complete a 3K, lose 15 pounds, or learn that really awesome song on the guitar that you keep meaning to do.



Tracking

We all know how forgetful you can be at times. Life Quest will allow you to keep a record of your completed quests as well as upload a photo of your victory. Keep an accurate log of your current progress and have a recordable history of all your accomplishments.

Motivation

Completing quests will earn you points. Points will give you levels. Levels will unlock flair and badges. Become a show off.
